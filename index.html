Workflow

Get In Touch

Use the Contact Us form at the bottom to write brief of your requirements. Try to be as specific as possible and include your budget and timeframe that you want. Alternatively, you can write to us directly at inquiry@DeveloperHired.com but this process will make the response rate slower. We recommend the contact form for faster query reply.


Request Under Review

Once you are done, sit tight while are looking at your requirements and we will get back to you as soon as we can in your email address provided. If we require additional information, we will ask them in the reply email. To make the communication process easier, we may ask for skype, telegram or any other sort of chat platform contact details so that we can discuss more about your project while we both are online.


Work Is Done

As soon as both of us agree with budget and timeframe mentioned, you will be given unique link to put in the payment in third party escrow website(you will be allowed to review about the third party website when provided to stay safe from fraud) and we will start working on your project. Once finished, we will show you proof of work and deliver you the work at your hand. Once you finish checking everything, you will be requested to release the funds escrowed in the third party website. If you are happy with the work, we become happy and our project completes successfully!
